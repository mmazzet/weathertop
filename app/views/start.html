Welcome to WeatherTop

This app allows you to submit weather reports from your station, capturing readings from the station at a specific time.

What is WeatherTop?

A visual tool for working with weather data and timely tracking of dangerous phenomena

How does WeatherTop work?

WeatherTop allows you to submit weather reports from your station, capturing readings from the station at a specific time

Weather parameters

Track the main weather parameters: Temperature, Wind speed, precipitations

How to start

It is easy to start working with the Dashboard. There are 5 steps:

  1. Sign up to our service. Alternatively, please log in with existing WeatherTop username and password.
  2. Go to the Dashboard and add the name of the Station, its latitude and longitude.
  3. Click on the "Folder" icon to open the Station Dashboard. Set up the conditions of your first reading by entering Weather Code, Temperature, Wind speed and Pressure
  4. Click on Submit Report to send the readings
  5. You can add new readings or delete existing ones.
